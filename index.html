Venice

Enjoy a gondola ride, visit St. Mark's Basilica, explore Doge's Palace, and relax in Piazza San Marco. Venture to Murano and Burano, and savor local italian cuisine. Discover the iconic Rialto Bridge and hidden canals for an unforgettable experience.

Cartagena

Explore the colorful Old Town, take a historic tour of the walled city, visit the impressive Castillo San Felipe and relax on beautiful beaches. The city's vibrant culture, colonial architecture, and delicious Caribbean cuisine make it a fantastic destination.

Porto

Explore the charming Ribeira district along the Douro River, visit the historic wine cellars in Gaia, admire the stunning Igreja de Francisco, and stroll across the iconic Dom Bridge. Don't miss the Livraria Lello, one of the world's most beautiful bookstores.
